Milan - IIT Hyderabad

About Milan

Milan is annual inter-hostel championship of IIT Hyderabad aimed at building community, teamwork, and friendly competition among students. It includes various sports, cultural and tech events like football, cricket, dance, music, debates, and quizzes. Each hostel sends its best participants to compete for points towards the overall championship. The event ends with a grand awards ceremony that honors individual and team achievements, creating strong bonds through shared experiences and spirited rivalry.
